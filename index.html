Cambodia

Cambodia is a country located in Southeast Asia, bordered by Thailand, Laos, Vietnam, and the Gulf of Thailand. It is known for its rich history, particularly the Khmer Empire, which built the famous Angkor Wat temple complex. The country has a diverse culture influenced by Buddhism, which is the predominant religion, and has a population of over 16 million people.
